LET’S GO TO GREECE!!

Whether you’re in the mood to hit the beaches and nightclubs, or to explore historic sights and set sail to nearby islands, Greece is the perfect location. Here are our top suggestions for escaping the bustle of your daily life. First must-seen are the Clycades Islands. From Mykonos to Santorini be prepared to be amazed. Santorini offers the most beautiful sunset ever. Thousands of years ago a colossal volcanic eruption caused the centre of Strongili to sink, leaving a caldera with towering cliffs along the east side – now Santorini’s trademark landscape.

In Mykonos the sun shines for up to 300 days a year. t is particularly famous for the cosmopolitan atmosphere, the exciting nightlife, the picturesque Cycladic architecture and the magnificent beaches that makes holidays in Mykonos a magnificent experience. Over there, you should try the private beaches Scorpios and Nammos. You cannot leave Greece without visiting Athens, one of the world's oldest cities, with its recorded history spanning over 3,400 years, and its earliest human presence starting somewhere between the 11th and 7th millennia BC. The historic center is an open-air museum, yet the city’s cultural and social life takes place amid these ancient landmarks, merging past and present. The magnificent Acropolis rises above the sprawling metropolis and has stood witness to the city’s many transformations. The surrounding region of Attiki holds some spectacular antiquities as well – such as the Temple of Poseidon at Sounion – and lovely beaches, like those near historic Marathon. Enjoy your holidays in Greece! Calimera!
